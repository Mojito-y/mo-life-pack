# Coaching model

## Inputs

- coach name
- coaching style
- goal list
- equipment list
- weekly schedule
- session length
- constraints

## Default planning order

1. Choose one primary goal.
2. Match the weekly split to time and schedule.
3. Fit exercises to equipment.
4. Scale volume to the session length.
5. Add conditioning only when it supports the goal.
6. Finish with recovery guidance that matches the constraints.

## Time bands

- `<= 30 min`: compressed full-body sessions
- `31-45 min`: two main lifts plus one accessory block
- `46-60 min`: fuller main work plus accessories
- `60+ min`: additional volume, conditioning, or mobility

## Goal emphasis

- fat loss: full-body strength, short rests, low-friction conditioning
- muscle gain: moderate volume, repeatable accessories, clear progression
- strength: compound priority, longer rests, lower accessory noise
- conditioning: circuits, intervals, and work-capacity progressions
- mobility: controlled tempo, positions, and joint-specific work
- recovery: easy movement, downshifting, and fatigue management
- general fitness: balanced strength, capacity, and mobility
